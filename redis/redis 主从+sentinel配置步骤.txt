1.构建主节点
redis-server --daemonize yes --appendonly yes --maxmemory-policy volatile-lru --protected-mode no --notify-keyspace-events Eglx
2.构建slave节点
redis-server --daemonize yes --appendonly yes --maxmemory-policy volatile-lru --protected-mode no --notify-keyspace-events Eglx --port 6379 --slaveof [masterip] [masterport]
3.进入主节点，构建sentinel
redis-sentinel ./sentinel.conf

建议: sentinel 节点数为奇数个
